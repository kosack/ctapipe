CameraFrame¶

class ctapipe.coordinates.CameraFrame(*args, **kwargs)[source]¶

Bases: astropy.coordinates.BaseCoordinateFrame

Camera coordinate frame. The camera frame is a simple physical cartesian frame, describing the 2 dimensional position of objects in the focal plane of the telescope Most Typically this will be used to describe the positions of the pixels in the focal plane
